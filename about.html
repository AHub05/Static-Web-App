Phonebook for your who, what, when, where needs

Recently I have been on a big kick to play Stardew Valley, but while playing I keep running into the issue of not knowing where to find certain villagers. This page is to put all the information I know about the villagers into one page with easy readability of the information I need.

I chose to use cards and rows for display of character information because I find it appealing and familiar from looking at rosters and trading cards.
